Energy2028, explained

Graphics and Development by ZACH LEVITT

Reporting by AMELIA POLLARD

On January 29, 2019, Middlebury College announced an ambitious plan to combat the threat of climate change. The plan included three major commitments: transition the College to 100% renewable energy sources, significantly reduce energy consumption and divest the College's $1 billion endowment from fossil fuel investments.

However, questions remain about how the College will achieve these goals, and what it will mean for its students, staff and faculty. Through this project, the Campus is committing to critically reporting on the progress of the College in meeting these goals, and offering students context, background and a deeper understanding of how this process will work.
